Mahila Swavalamban Scheme


Description :



The primary objective of the scheme is to provide economic upliftment to women living below the poverty line. This scheme has been implemented since year 1996. Women between the ages of 21 to 50 years whose annual income is in the range of Rs.1,50,000 / - in urban areas and Rs.1,20,000 / - in rural areas are eligible to avail benefits under this scheme. Eligible women can get a loan of up to Rs. 2,00,000 / - through a bank


Eligibility Criteria :



Women between the ages of 21 to 50 years whose annual income is in the range of Rs. 1,50,000 / - in urban areas and Rs. 1,20,000 / - in rural areas are eligible to avail benefits under this scheme.
