Gujarat Women Economic Development Corporation Ltd


Description :



Gujarat Women Economic Development Corporation Ltd. In order for women to become economically self-reliant, to contribute to the economic development of the family, women who need employment-oriented, trade-oriented and technical training, including lineage traditional home industry, are provided financial support under the bankable scheme to start an industry or business. And these women are provided a direct market through the exhibition co-sale scheme for the sale of their products.

One day “ Women Awareness Camp” and five days “ Women Entrepreneurship Development Training” are conducted by Indian Entrepreneurship Development Institute (EDII) from year 2020-21 . Women's awareness camp provides understanding to women with basic information to set up their own business. Under this, 177 women awareness camps have been organized. In which 8261 women have been benefited. And in a five-day women's entrepreneurship development training scheme, women are trained to set up their own businesses and given hand-holding support for 10 months. Under this, 40 trainings have been organized and 1352 women have been benefited.

Thus as a result of hand-holding support by the Indian Entrepreneurship Development Institute (EDII), 225 trained women entrepreneurs applied under GWEDC's Mahila Swavalban Yojana. So far 12 women have received funds under Mahila Swavalamban Yojana and PMEGP. 11 women got jobs after skill development training . Also, through this intervention, 546 women have started their own micro enterprises with an initial investment of Rs.211.00 lakh. Thus, Gujarat Women's Economic Development Corporation Ltd. It has been training since its early stages. Through which the trained women have become self-reliant by getting assistance under the bankable scheme of the corporation. Besides, direct market facility is provided for sale of self-produced items by women entrepreneurs under the Demonstration Co-Sales Scheme. For which 1783 women entrepreneurs have been registered till now on this portal http://mela.gwedc.gov.in . Women entrepreneurs are participating in exhibition co sales fair. Also these women are giving employment to many other women under their hands

Gujarat Women Economic Development Corporation Ltd. The training portal is being finalized under the General Training Scheme. In which the portal will be linked to the women's self-help portal and the exhibition co-sale portal. So that the women trained under the General Training Scheme will become self-reliant and new employment opportunities will be created. Thus, Gujarat Women Economic Development Corporation Ltd. There is a “ one stop center ”


Eligibility Criteria :



Women between the ages of 21 to 50 years whose annual income is in the range of Rs. 1,50,000 / - in urban areas and Rs. 1,20,000 / - in rural areas are eligible to avail benefits under this scheme.
